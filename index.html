Special Experiences

Meet the Dolphins!

If you’ve always wanted to meet friendly dolphins and say “Hi!” in person, now’s your chance! Come meet these amazing marine mammals, learn more about them and discover how you can protect them!

Note: This is a non-contact experience.

Price: SGD15 Duration: 15 minutes Capacity: 5 people Sessions: 11am, 12 noon, 2pm and 3pm Terms & Conditions apply.

Up-Close Encounter

Get closer, much closer to our marine animals with the Up-Close Encounter pass! This brand-new premium experience lets you see an amazing diversity of marine life in a different light.

  • Exclusive entry to the Open Ocean Habitat Up-Close Zone, where you’ll gaze into a spellbinding marine world right next to one of the region’s largest viewing panels.
  • Access to Ocean Dome, where a breathtaking panorama surrounds you as you look up to fish gliding above.
  • Enjoy priority entry into S.E.A. Aquarium.
  • Meet the Dolphins to learn more about how we care for these fascinating animals.
  • Get set for your close encounters of the marine kind today.

All Annual Passholders will also experience the Up-Close Encounter (excluding Meet the Dolphins) for free!

This experience will not be available till further notice.

Ocean Dreams

An unparalleled sleepover experience featuring over 100,000 marine animals As the day ends, night life begins under the sea! Witness nocturnal sharks becoming active, animalsbehaving in new ways, sea jellies communicating with one another and more. Drift into slumber as you camp in a tent at the Open Ocean Gallery with countless marine animals gliding by, and wake up to aquarists interacting with Manta Rays in a special feeding session. See the sea in a new light in this sleepover to remember!

This experience will not be available till further notice.

Note: This is an overnight event. Dates are subject to change and availability. Time: 6:30pm - 10:00am (Following Day)

Contact Us: Email public.programmes@rwsentosa.com

Reservations must be confirmed two weeks prior to scheduled event.

Programme Highlights

  • Camp out in the Open Ocean Gallery – featuring one of the world’s largest viewing panels.
  • Experience a guided tour with interactive learning – discover how the marine world is different at night and have fun at sensory learning stations.
  • Learn about pollution, sustainable seafood and what you can do to protect our seas.
  • Wake up to meet our divers for a special photo opportunity.
  • Witness a special Manta Ray feeding session.
